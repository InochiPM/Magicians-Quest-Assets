Operating Environment

These plug-ins support Adobe Photoshop CS5 (12.0.1), Adobe Photoshop CS4 (11.0.1), Adobe Photoshop CS3 (10.0.1), and Adobe Photoshop CS2 (9.0.2) for Windows XP (32-bit); and Adobe Photoshop CS5 64-bit (12.0.1) and Adobe Photoshop CS4 64-bit (11.0.1) for theWindows 7 (64-bit).

Operations are not guaranteed for other versions.
Note: Adobe Photoshop CS3 (10.0.1) and Adobe Photoshop CS2 (9.0.2) are not guaranteed to run on a 64-bit operating system.
Note: Adobe Photoshop CS5 64-bit and Adobe Photoshop CS4 64-bit are not guaranteed to run on the 64-bit version of Windows XP.
